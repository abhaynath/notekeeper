Basic Motion Tracking

  1. Add the clip in the timeline.
  2. Search Motion Tracker effect and drop it on the clip in the timeline.
  3. In effects panel, you should see the Motion Tracker details.
  5. Drag the red selection around the object you want to track.
  6. Click Analyze to Apply Effect button. It should take some time to process.
  7. Once analysis is done, you should see the path of the object in the video like this,
  9. Add a new Title Clip above the video clip.
  10. Select the video clip in the timeline, and open effects panel. In motion tracking effects option, click Copy all keyframes to clipboard
  12. Select the Title clip. Apply Transform Effect to the title clip.
  13. In Transform Effects of Title Clip, select Import Keyframes from clipboard
  16. Play the Project. You should see title moving along the tracked object.
  17. You can disable the red tracking marks in Motion tracker effects panel by setting Shape Width to 0.
