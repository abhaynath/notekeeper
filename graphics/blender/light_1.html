3Point lighting setup

There are 3 main lights required in 3 Point light setup.

  1. Key light main source of light in the scene. Point light (~1000W)
  2. Fill light Dimmest source of light in the scene. Mainly used to reduce the shadow formed by keylight. Point light (~200W)
  3. Rim light Brightest light in the scene. To create the shiny outline on the model. Area lamp (~50000W)
