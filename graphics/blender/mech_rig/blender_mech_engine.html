Piston Rigging

  1. We need 2 seperate Armatures to rig the engine. Piston Rig and Crank Rig

  2. Piston Rig consists of 3 bones. Stretch bone, piston bone and Shaft bone.

    IK Stretch should be set to 1.

  3. Add IK Constraint on Shaft Bone.

  4. We need shaft bone because if we add IK constraint to crack bone, crank bone will rotate around its center. And Shaft will always point to Crank. Since Crank will always stationary, we wont be able to see and animation. Thats why we need one extra bone Shaft IK which will rotate around the circumference of the Crank bone.

  5. This is how it will animate after rigging its done.
